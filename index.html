The framework

Five categories.
One clear picture.

Every consultation — regardless of tier — is assessed across the same five photo-observed categories. This is The Skin Sage Wellness Framework. Your scores are based on what is visible in your photographs and what you declare at the time of assessment.

01
Surface Skin Condition

The visible appearance of your skin surface — hydration signs, barrier indicators and overall surface presentation.

Photo observed
02
Pigmentation & Tone

Evenness of skin tone, visible pigmentation, post-inflammatory marks and distribution of colour across the face.

Photo observed
03
Texture & Pore Appearance

Surface texture, pore visibility, smoothness and any structural irregularity visible from photographic assessment.

Photo observed
04
Visible Skin Clarity

Visible inflammation, active breakout activity, stability and the overall clarity of the skin as observed in photographs.

Photo observed
05
Radiance & Luminosity

The visible brightness, luminosity and radiance of the skin surface as presented in natural light photographs.

Photo observed

Questions

Frequently asked.

How can you assess my skin accurately from photographs?+
We ask for up to four photographs — front facing, right profile, left profile and a close-up of your primary concern area — combined with a detailed intake form covering your lifestyle, current routine and skin history. Together these give us a thorough observational picture of your skin. All assessments are educational and observational, never a clinical measurement and never a replacement for your GP or dermatologist.
Do you recommend specific products or brands?+
No. We review the products you already have and tell you honestly whether they are suited to your skin. When something new would genuinely help, we guide you on what ingredient to look for and why — not a specific brand or product. We have no brand affiliations and no commercial agenda beyond your skin health.
How do I book my Reiki session?+
Your Reiki session is included in the Unlocked package and available as a standalone addition to any consultation for £85. Once your report is delivered, you will receive a Whereby link and scheduling options by email. Sessions are conducted virtually — no app or download needed, just a link that opens in your browser. Scheduling is by mutual agreement within 30 days of your report delivery. The Reiki session is provided for relaxation and energetic wellbeing support only. It is not a medical treatment and no health claims are made.
What format does my report arrive in?+
Your report arrives as a professionally presented locked PDF document delivered directly to your email inbox within 24 hours (The Score and Reveal) or 48 hours (Decoded and Unlocked) of receiving your photographs. Yours to keep, download and return to whenever you need it.
Is this a medical service?+
No. The Skin Sage is an educational and informational skin wellness service. It does not constitute medical advice, a clinical diagnosis or a treatment plan. Nothing provided through this service should replace the advice of a qualified medical professional. If anything observed in your photographs or intake information falls outside the scope of aesthetic wellness, you will be advised to seek GP or dermatologist assessment.
Can I upgrade my consultation?+
Yes — and your payment always counts. If you start with The Score at £25 and decide you want Reveal, you pay just £40 more. Reveal to Decoded is £80 more. Reveal to Unlocked is £230 more. Decoded to Unlocked is £150 more. You never pay twice for the same thing.
How do I submit my photos?+
Once your intake form is submitted and payment is received, you will receive a secure photo upload link by email. Photos are uploaded securely — never via standard email. We require front facing, right profile and left profile photographs in natural daylight, without makeup, without filters, with hair pulled back. Decoded and Unlocked also require a close-up photograph of your main area of concern.
What is The Inner Sage?+
The Inner Sage is our community — a space for honest skin education, ingredient guidance, seasonal skin knowledge and real skin journeys. Created by an aesthetic practitioner and holistic wellness practitioner, it is where skin knowledge meets inner wisdom. Join by requesting your invitation on this page.

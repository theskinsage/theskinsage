Frequently asked questions

Questions we get asked.

No gatekeeping here either. We answer everything honestly.

How can you assess my skin accurately from photographs?+
We ask for four photographs in specific conditions — front facing, left profile, right profile and a close up of your primary concern area — combined with a detailed intake form. Together these give us a thorough observational picture of your skin. Our assessments use the same clinical visual skills that practitioners use to read skin — indicators of barrier function, hydration, pigmentation, texture, circulation and vitality. We are transparent that our assessments are observational and educational — not clinical measurements and never a replacement for your GP or dermatologist.
Do you recommend specific products or brands?+
We review the products you already have and tell you honestly whether they are suited to your skin. When something new would genuinely help, we guide you on what ingredient to look for and why — not a specific brand or product. You choose what to buy. We have no brand affiliations and no agenda beyond your skin. No gatekeeping.
Is this the same as seeing a dermatologist?+
No — and we are always transparent about that. The Skin Sage provides educational and informational guidance based on observational assessment of your photographs and intake form. It does not constitute a medical consultation, clinical diagnosis or treatment plan. If anything is identified outside the scope of aesthetic wellness you will be advised to consult your GP or a dermatologist. Your health always comes first.
What format does my report arrive in?+
Your report arrives as a professionally presented locked PDF document delivered directly to your email inbox. Locked means the content is protected — yours to read, download, print and keep forever. Not a link that expires. Not a document someone else can edit. Completely yours.
What happens if you see something concerning in my photos?+
If we observe anything in your photographs that falls outside the scope of aesthetic wellness — a mole, an unusual skin change or anything that warrants medical attention — we will flag it clearly in your report and advise you to consult your GP or a dermatologist before beginning any new skincare routine. Your safety always takes priority over the consultation.
What if I wear a hijab — how do I take my photos?+
Your face is what we need to see — not your hair. You can wear your hijab exactly as you normally would for all four photographs. What matters is that your face is clearly visible in natural daylight with no makeup and no filters. The Skin Sage is for every person. No exceptions.
What is the Reiki session and what does it involve?+
The Reiki session is a guided 30 minute session provided for relaxation and energetic balance. It is a separate wellbeing offering — not part of the skin consultation and not a medical treatment. No health claims are made. Available to add to any consultation and included in Unlocked.
Is my data and my photos safe?+
Your data and photographs are handled securely in accordance with UK GDPR. Your photos are used only to produce your consultation report and are not shared, sold or used for any other purpose. You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@theskinsage.com. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
Can I upgrade my tier after I have paid?+
Yes. If you start with The Score or Reveal and decide you would like the full Decoded or Unlocked experience, simply email hello@theskinsage.com and we will arrange an upgrade. You pay only the difference between your original tier and the new one.
Do I need to be based in the UK?+
No. The Skin Sage is a fully virtual service. You can be anywhere in the world. Everything is delivered digitally to your inbox. The only requirement is that you can complete the intake form and submit your four photographs.
How is this different from just googling my skin concerns?+
Google gives you generic information written for everyone. We give you a personal assessment of your specific skin, your specific products and your specific routine — from someone with clinical expertise and no agenda to sell you anything. The information you find online is for everyone. Your report is written for you.
What if I have a medical skin condition?+
If you have a diagnosed medical skin condition such as eczema, psoriasis, rosacea or acne, please note this on your intake form. We can provide educational guidance on your routine and ingredients within the scope of aesthetic wellness — but we always recommend consulting your GP or dermatologist for the management of medical conditions. Your health always comes first.

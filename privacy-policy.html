Section 02

What data we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data depending on which service you use:

When you complete an intake form

  • Your preferred name and pronouns
  • Your email address
  • Your age range
  • Your skin concerns and skin history
  • Information about your current skincare products and routine
  • Lifestyle information — smoking status, sleep, exercise, diet, stress levels, hydration, hormonal factors, pillowcase hygiene
  • Diagnosed skin conditions and any relevant medications (health-related data)
  • Information about aesthetic treatments you have received
  • Known allergies
  • Declaration of what is on your face in your photographs
  • Consent confirmations

When you submit photographs

  • Photographic images of your face — front facing, right profile, left profile, and close-up of your main concern area
  • Photographs may constitute biometric data under UK GDPR

When you contact us

  • Your name and email address
  • The content of your message

When you join The Inner Sage community

  • Your first name and email address

Technical data collected automatically

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website

We do not collect payment card data directly. All payments are processed by our payment processor and we never see or store your card details.

Section 04

Our lawful basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, we must have a lawful basis for processing your personal data. We rely on the following:

Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b))

Where processing is necessary to perform the consultation service you have purchased, or to take steps at your request before entering into a contract with us.

Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f))

Where processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests — such as responding to enquiries, improving our service, and protecting our business — provided these are not overridden by your interests or rights.

Consent (Article 6(1)(a))

Where you have given us clear, specific and informed consent — for example, to receive The Inner Sage community communications. You can withdraw consent at any time by contacting us at hello@theskinsage.com or by using the unsubscribe link in any email.

Legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c))

Where processing is necessary to comply with a legal obligation to which we are subject.

Section 05

Special category data — health information and photographs

Important: Some of the data we collect is classified as "special category data" under UK GDPR because it relates to your health. This includes information about diagnosed skin conditions, medications, hormonal factors, allergies and your facial photographs. Special category data receives additional legal protection.

We process special category data only with your explicit consent, which you provide when you tick the consent boxes on your intake form before submitting. You have the right to withdraw this consent at any time.

We collect this data solely for the purpose of producing your consultation report. We do not use it for any other purpose without your explicit further consent.

Your photographs may constitute biometric data under UK GDPR Article 9. We process them only on the basis of your explicit consent, solely for the purpose of conducting your photo-observed skin assessment.

We apply heightened security measures to all special category data. It is stored on secure, encrypted platforms and is accessible only to the practitioner producing your report.

Section 09

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the following rights in relation to your personal data. These rights are not absolute and may be subject to certain exemptions, but we will always respond to your request within one month of receiving it.

Right of access
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you (a Subject Access Request). We will provide this free of charge within one month.
Right to rectification
You can ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.
Right to erasure
You can ask us to delete your personal data. We will comply unless we have a legal obligation or legitimate reason to retain it.
Right to restrict processing
You can ask us to pause the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances while a dispute is resolved.
Right to data portability
You can request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format to transfer to another provider.
Right to object
You can object to processing based on legitimate interests. We will stop unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds.
Right to withdraw consent
Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time. This does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.
Right to complain
You have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk or by calling 0303 123 1113 if you believe we have mishandled your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@theskinsage.com. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request. We will respond within one month. If your request is complex, we may extend this by a further two months and will notify you accordingly.

Section 10

Cookies and tracking technologies

Our website uses only strictly necessary cookies required for the site to function. We do not currently use advertising cookies, tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies that require your consent.

GitHub Pages, which hosts our website, may set technical cookies as part of its standard hosting infrastructure. These are strictly necessary for website functionality and do not identify you personally.

We do not use Google Analytics or any similar tracking service that would require a cookie consent banner.

If we introduce any non-essential cookies in the future, we will update this policy and implement a cookie consent mechanism in accordance with the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and UK GDPR.

Section 11

Children

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18.

If you are under 18 and wish to use our services, you must have the consent of a parent or legal guardian, who must complete the intake form on your behalf and provide their own consent.

If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 18 without appropriate parental consent, we will delete that data immediately.

If you believe we have collected data from a child under 18 without appropriate consent, please contact us immediately at hello@theskinsage.com.

Section 12

How we protect your data

We take the security of your personal data seriously, particularly given that we process health-related information and facial photographs. We implement the following measures:

  • All client data and photographs are stored on secure, password-protected platforms with encrypted storage
  • Access to client data is restricted to the practitioner producing your report
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • We do not store photographs or health data in standard email — all such data is handled via secure platforms
  • We regularly review our security practices and update them as necessary
  • We do not retain data beyond its stated retention period

Data breach procedure

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the ICO within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by UK GDPR Article 33. If the breach is likely to result in a high risk to your rights and freedoms, we will also notify you directly without undue delay.
